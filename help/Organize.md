- **Reparent:** drag a card onto another to make it a child.
- **Collapse / expand:** double-click a card, or use the toolbar's collapse/expand.
- **Colour & layout:** set per-card in the side panel; children can inherit.
- **Duplicate / delete:** from the toolbar's selected-card section.
