- **Pan:** drag the empty canvas (one finger on touch), or `Space` + drag.
- **Zoom:** pinch, or ctrl / ⌘ + scroll.
- **Fit / focus:** press `F` — fits everything, or frames the selected card.
- **Find:** press `/` and type to jump to a card.
