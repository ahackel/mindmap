This is a **local-first mindmap editor** for a folder of Markdown notes.

- **One file per card.** Each card is a Markdown (`.md`) note: the title is the filename, the body is your text.
- **Branches are the tree.** Each card hangs off its parent — that link is all that's stored.
- Your map lives **on this device** and reopens automatically. No account, no cloud, no lock-in.

## Take the tour

The cards around this one each cover a topic. **Expand one** (double-click, or press `X`) to reveal its details, then go deeper into the nested cards.
