To move a map between devices, use the sidebar:

- **Export .zip** (in a map's ⋮ menu) bundles the open map into one file.
- **Import .zip** adds it as a **new map** — nothing gets mixed into your existing ones.

There's no built-in cloud sync — zip is the portable, no-lock-in way to carry your map.

## Single cards

- **Drag `.md` files onto the canvas** to import them as cards — drop them on a card to add them as its children. Parent links between the dropped notes are kept.
- **Drag cards out**: hold `⌥`/`Alt` and drag a card out of the window to your file manager — one card saves as a `.md` file, a subtree or multi-selection as a `.zip` (Chrome/Edge only).
- **Export button**: the export button in the sidebar's Actions row downloads the selected cards (with their subtrees) as one `.zip` — works in every browser.
