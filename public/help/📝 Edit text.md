- **Rename:** slow-click the title (a second click on the selected card), press `F2`, or use the rename button.

- **Edit the note:** slow-click a card's body, or press `E`, to type the note right on the card. Press `↓` while renaming to drop into the body.

- `Enter` adds a line · `Esc` cancels · click away to keep. **It autosaves.**
