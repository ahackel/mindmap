Open the sidebar with the **🧠 button**:

- **Click a map** to open it. The current one is highlighted.
- **＋ New map** starts a fresh one.
- The **⋮ menu** (or right-click) on a map offers **Rename**, **Export .zip** and **Delete**.

Maps saved on this device are private to this browser and reopen automatically.
