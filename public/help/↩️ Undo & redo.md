Made a mistake? Every edit — create, delete, move, recolour, rename — is undoable.

- **Undo:** `Ctrl` / `⌘` + `Z`
- **Redo:** `Ctrl` / `⌘` + `Shift` + `Z`

Experiment freely; you can always step back.
